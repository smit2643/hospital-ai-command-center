# Executive Summary

## Vision
Hospital AI Command Center is a digital operations platform that modernizes hospital paperwork and approvals into a secure, audit-ready, AI-assisted workflow.

## What It Delivers
- Fast onboarding of doctors and patients through role-based workflows
- Structured data extraction from lab report images using free OCR tooling
- Tokenized document-signature flow with signed PDF artifact storage
- Integrity verification through SHA-256 hash tracking
- Complete action traceability through audit logs

## Business Impact
- Reduces manual effort in document intake and data transcription
- Increases compliance confidence via immutable action trails
- Improves care coordination by unifying doctor-patient document operations
- Creates a scalable base for future claims, EHR, and analytics integrations

## Why It Can Win
- Solves a real operational pain point, not a toy use case
- Demonstrates full-stack depth: UX, backend, async processing, security, deployment
- Shows practical AI usage with measurable operational output
- Ready to demo today and extend tomorrow
